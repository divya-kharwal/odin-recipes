Vada




Description

Vada is a popular South Indian savory snack and are usually served hot with coconut chutney and sambar.


Ingredients

  • Urad daal
  • Green chilies
  • Salt
  • Sliced onions
  • Oil
  • Ginger
  • Curry leaves

Steps

  1. Soak urad dal for 4â€“5 hours, drain, and grind into a thick, fluffy batter.
  2. Add chopped green chilies, ginger, curry leaves, cumin, onion, and salt to the batter.
  3. Wet your hands, take some batter, make a small ball, and poke a hole in the center.
  4. Deep fry vadas in hot oil on medium flame until they turn golden and crisp.
  5. Enjoy with coconut chutney and hot sambar!
