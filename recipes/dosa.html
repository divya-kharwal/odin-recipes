Onion Rava Masala Dosa




Description

This dosa is made of semolina and is best served with coconut chutney and hot sambar.


Ingredients

    Rava Dosa Batter

  • Rava
  • Water
  • Salt

    • Masala (Potato Filling)

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Chopped onions
  • Green chilli

Steps

  1. Make Batter - Mix rava, rice flour, maida, spices, onion, herbs, and water (make it thin).
  2. Prepare Masala - Sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, onion, turmeric → add mashed potato & salt.
  3. Heat Tawa - Pour thin batter from height, no spreading needed.
  4. Cook Dosa - Drizzle oil, cook till crisp and golden.
  5. Add Masala & Serve - Place potato masala, fold dosa, and serve hot with chutney.
